About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in climate science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in the department of Earth, Atmosphere and Plantery Sciences.

My thesis advisor is John Marshall.

Research interests

I am interested in a wide range of topics, including:

  • The sensitivity of the climate to radiative perturbations
  • Multiple equilibria in the climate system and tipping points
  • Southern ocean dynamics and biogeochemistry
